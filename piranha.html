About Piranha

Piranha is a software tool for the analysis of nanopore sequencing data, specifically designed for the identification and characterization of poliovirus sequences. It provides a user-friendly interface for processing and analyzing sequencing data, enabling researchers to quickly and accurately identify poliovirus strains and monitor their evolution.
